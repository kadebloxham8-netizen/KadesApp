Hello

Wave hand near head. Practice: wave 3 times.

👍

Good / Yes

Thumb up. Practice: hold 2 seconds.

🙌

Thank you

Hand from chin outward. Practice: repeat slowly.

🤝

Friend

Hook index fingers together then swap. Practice slowly.
