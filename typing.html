Typing Lesson

Instructions

Click "Start Lesson" to begin. Type each word shown. Press Enter to move to the next word. If you make a mistake, it will say "Please try again".

You can switch between App Words and Non-App Words using the dropdown below.

Tips for Typing

  • Keep your eyes on the word, not the keyboard.
  • Use the correct finger placement.
  • Focus on accuracy first, speed comes later.
  • Use hints if needed, then try again.

Progress

Track your WPM (Words Per Minute) and accuracy as you practice. Repeat lessons to improve!
